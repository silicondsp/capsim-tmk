
execute capsim by typing(MSYS2):

winpty   ../../BIN/MSYS2/capsim.exe the_topology.t


Topologies are:

carrierRecovery.t :
	DSBSC modulation with carrier recovery

ssb.t:
	Single sideband modulation with hilbert transforms

fmmod.t:
	Simple FM modulation.

Results stored in *.tim for time domain and *.fre for 
frequency domain plots in text mode.
